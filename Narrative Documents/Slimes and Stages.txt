Types of slimes
Slime Colors
* Red
   * Stretchy and Self-Hardening
   * Used to make shapes of various sizes to act as temporary platforms
* Green
   * Sticky
   * Climb walls, slow enemies
* Blue
   * Slippery
   * Speed build up for long jumps, make enemies fall off a ledge.


Slime Enemy types
* Goopa
   * Base slimes, do almost nothing, run into player to deal damage.
* Bosses
   * More powerful slimes at the end of the stage, have unique attacks
   * Most, if not all, bosses will have 9 hitpoints. Every 3 attacks the boss will do a powerful move that the player must avoid.


        Bosses
* OBSOOOTM
   * Aggressive Goopa stuck in a bucket, special attack is a large jump followed by a slam into the player.
   * Special attack consists of consecutive jumps across the arena, the player must avoid by running beneath OBSOOO between each jump.
* Barney
   * Spider like slime that hangs from the ceiling
   * Throws slime at the player that hurts and slows upon hitting the player
   * After three hits he throws a large amount of slime over the half of the stage that will hurt the player and is too thick to clean 
   * After both sides of the stage have been covered the boss will fall to the ground swinging its arms/tentacles around which if the player gets hit by will do damage
